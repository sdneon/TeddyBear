Teddy Bear
==========
Teddy bear shows the hour and points out the minutes with a hand. (Colour, Analogue).

Mod of [Old Forgies](https://github.com/tdsbtoes/OldFogies) & [No Hands](https://github.com/sdneon/NoHands).
